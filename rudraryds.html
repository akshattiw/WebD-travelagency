Tips for your Time in Kashi

Dress Modestly

Especially when visiting temples (Kashi Vishwanath) and Ghats. Cover shoulders and knees.

Beware of Monkeys

Keep food and glasses hidden in the old city lanes. They are mischievous!

Best Time to Visit

October to March is pleasant. Summers (April-June) can be extremely hot.

Walk or Ride

Cars cannot enter the main Ghat areas and Old Galis. Walking or 2-wheelers are your best friends.
